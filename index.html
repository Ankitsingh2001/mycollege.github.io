Vice Chancellor's Message

Prof. Annpurna Nautiyal

Hemvati Nandan Bahuguna Garhwal University named after the Late Sri Hemvati Nandan Bahuguna, was established in 1973 after a mass movement launched to fulfill higher education needs of this remote and geographically difficult region. Since then the University has travelled a long way and made laudable progress to achieve the goals envisioned by the esteemed son of the soil “Hemvati”
